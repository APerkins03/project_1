Princess Dash


Use the left and right arrows to move the Princess!

Collect the slippers and the crowns but be careful not to touch the frogs or the ogres. If you collect the slippers you get 5 points, crowns are 10! If you touch a frog you lose a life, if you touch and ogre you lose the game. Good Luck!
